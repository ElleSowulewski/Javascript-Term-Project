These puzzle games are made with HTML5 and JQuery.

These are 3 puzzle games where a user drags the pieces
to put a picture together.

Puzzle 1 - Easy 3x3 - Dewdrop Picture
Puzzle 2 - Medium 4x4 - Cherry Blossom Picture
Puzzle 3 - Hard 5x5 - Feather Picture

Future release plans:
